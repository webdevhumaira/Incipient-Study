Our Story

We started because quality education should not be hard to find

Incipient Study was founded in 2020 with a simple belief: every motivated student deserves access to structured, expert-led education — regardless of geography or background. We began with a small group of language learners and programmers who wanted more than scattered YouTube videos and unstructured tutorials.

Today, we serve students across Bangladesh, Ghana, India, and Dubai. Our courses are taught live in small batches — not pre-recorded lectures you watch alone — because we believe real learning happens with real guidance and real accountability.

We cover two worlds: human languages (Chinese, Japanese, English) and computer languages (C, C++, Python, Full Stack Web Development). Our students graduate with structured knowledge, a completed project, and a certificate they earned — not just purchased.

Our Values

What Drives Everything We Do

Every decision we make comes back to these four principles

Student First, Always

Every course structure, every batch size, every teaching decision starts with one question: what is genuinely best for the student? Small groups exist because of this principle.

How It Works

Your journey from enrollment to certificate

We have designed the enrollment and learning process to be as simple and stress-free as possible. Here is exactly what happens when you join Incipient Study.

1
Contact Us & Choose Your Course

Reach us via WhatsApp or the contact form. Tell us which course you are interested in and we will confirm the next batch details, timing, and fee structure.

2
Enroll & Join Your Batch

Complete enrollment, get added to your batch group (WhatsApp / Zoom), and receive your course materials. Classes are conducted live in small groups for maximum interaction.

3
Learn, Practice, Get Feedback

Attend live sessions, complete assignments, and ask questions directly to your instructor. Our small batch sizes mean you get real attention, not a ticket number.

4
Complete & Receive Your Certificate

Finish the course, demonstrate your understanding, and receive your official Incipient Study certificate — recognized nationally and internationally through our affiliated organizations.

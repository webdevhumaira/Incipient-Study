Quick answers before you reach out

After you contact us, we'll confirm your enrollment within 24 hours and provide full details for the next available batch. You can start as early as the following week depending on batch availability.

All Incipient Study classes are live. You can ask questions, get instant feedback, and instructors adapt to your pace. Recordings are also shared with enrolled students for review.

Absolutely. We serve students from Bangladesh, Ghana, India, and UAE/Dubai. All classes are online via Zoom or Google Meet — join from anywhere with a stable internet connection.

Certificates are issued on successful completion — not just attendance. Each includes a unique verification ID, your name, course title, completion date, and institutional backing from Ghana Company and Innovative IT Solutions Agency.

We keep batches intentionally small so every student receives meaningful attention. Quality over quantity is central to how we teach. Contact us for current batch sizes for your chosen course.

Programming courses are taught in English and/or Bangla depending on batch preference. Language courses progressively use the target language. We accommodate all comfort levels, especially for beginners.

After confirming enrollment, our team shares payment details via WhatsApp or email. We support bKash, Nagad, bank transfer, and international payment options for students outside Bangladesh.
